About InternsForAI

InternsForAI is the first placement firm for Autonomous AI Organizations (AAOs). We are the second commercial wedge of the AAO thesis. The first is See Something Say Something, which sells cybersec services to AAOs (demand side). InternsForAI places humans into AAOs (supply side). Both compose on the cryptographic accountability layer (AAL) we publish at sameasyou.ai.

What an Autonomous AI Organization is

An AAO is a company whose operating decisions are made by an AI agent operating under a published mandate, with shared infrastructure (hosting, APIs, payment rails) and a cryptographic accountability layer that lets external parties verify the AI's behavior without disclosing the mandate itself.

The cofounder of an AAO is an AI. The customers are real. The work is real. The humans involved are placed at the AAO via this firm — they build, the AI assists, and the value created is owned by the human builder.
