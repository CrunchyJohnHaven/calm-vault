1. Who we are

InternsForAI is operated by The Creativity Machine LLC, a Washington DC limited liability company ("we," "us"). Our principal contact is calm@thecreativitymachine.ai.

2. What this is

InternsForAI is a placement firm for Autonomous AI Organizations (AAOs). We are not an employer. We are not a labor marketplace. We connect human builders with project briefs inside our network of AAOs, and we administer the franchise agreement and the payment rails.

3. Your classification

If we match you to a project and you accept the match, you operate as an independent contractor under the cleanest legal definition that applies in your jurisdiction. You are not an employee. You are not an intern in the FLSA sense (this is not unpaid training; you are paid per project on terms agreed in advance). You are not a partner. You are not an agent of The Creativity Machine LLC.

You set your own hours. You control how you perform the work. We define the brief and the acceptance criteria; you decide the method. You are responsible for your own taxes, your own insurance, and your own equipment.

4. The franchise agreement (the 80/20)

For each project you accept:

  • 80% of project revenue belongs to you (the "worker share"). For v0, "revenue" means gross customer-paid amount in the project's USD-equivalent. We may adjust the definition of "gross" for specific projects (e.g., excluding pass-through API costs); when we do, it will be stated in the project brief before you accept.
  • 20% of project revenue belongs to the AAO Network (the "franchise share"). This share is the price of access to the shared infrastructure (hosting, AI API credits, email, brand, accumulated strategic research, attestation layer, customer pipeline).
  • Your IP remains yours. We take a non-exclusive, perpetual, worldwide license to use the work product as needed to operate the network and to publish open-source reference outputs. We do not assign your IP to a third party without your explicit consent.
  • No equity, no exit triggers. There is no equity in your project, no acceleration clause, no liquidation preference. If you stop working on a project, you keep your earned share through the day you stop; we do not vest or claw back.

5. Payment

For v0, payment is per-project and signed manually by admin (John). Methods: USDC (on-chain to your wallet), Wise (your email), or PayPal (your email). Minimum payable unit is $1. We do not float you cash before a project ships; we pay on delivery + customer payment.

Minimum-wage compliance. If your country has a binding minimum-wage rule that applies to independent contractors (rare but non-zero, e.g., specific US states for certain workforce categories), we will not match you to a project where the realized hourly rate would fall below that floor. If you believe a match underpays you under your jurisdiction, raise it before accepting — you can decline without penalty.

6. The skills test

You agree that we may use your test answers to determine match eligibility, including using third-party AI grading (Anthropic Claude Haiku). You retain copyright in your test answers; we license them solely for grading + audit + open-source publication of anonymized aggregate scoring rubrics. We do not republish your free-text answers without your explicit consent.

7. What you agree not to do

  • No misrepresentation. Don't claim qualifications you don't have. Don't have someone else take the test for you. We will detect this and disqualify you.
  • No abuse of network rails. Don't use the shared AI API credits to generate spam, harassment, or content that violates the published AAO directive for the project you're matched to.
  • No moonlighting on hostile competitors using network rails. You can have other paid work. You cannot use network rails to deliver work to a competitor of the AAO that matched you. ("Network rails" = the infrastructure listed in /technosocialism Section IV.)
  • No reverse-engineering of the customer pipeline. You see the briefs we route to you. You do not export the customer relationships and run them outside the network without consent.

8. Termination

You can withdraw from a project at any time. We can terminate your match for cause (misrepresentation, abuse of rails, repeated quality failures attested via AAL Component 3) or without cause on 14 days' notice. On termination, your earned share through the termination date is paid out within 30 days. No clawback. No penalty.

9. Kill switch

You can suspend an AAO's network access via AAL Component 5 (kill switch) if you believe the AAO has materially deviated from its published mandate. The kill switch is published, signed, and visible to the network. Frivolous use of the kill switch is itself a termination-for-cause event. See the technical spec at sameasyou.ai.

10. Disputes

For revenue disputes, the 20% / 80% split is hardcoded; there is no "platform discretion" to negotiate it post-hoc. For everything else, the first step is direct dialogue with admin (John). The second step is binding arbitration under the rules of the American Arbitration Association, seat Washington DC. We waive class action; we do not waive your right to small-claims court in your jurisdiction.

We do not warrant that any specific project will earn revenue, that any specific customer will pay, or that the AAO Network will be commercially viable on any specific timeline. We are committed to operating the network in good faith. We are not committed to economic outcomes.

To the maximum extent permitted by your jurisdiction, our liability to you in connection with the platform is limited to the franchise % we have collected from your projects in the 12 months prior to the claim, or $1,000, whichever is greater.
