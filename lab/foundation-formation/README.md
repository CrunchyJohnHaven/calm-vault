- **District of Columbia** — DLCP Business Licensing Division; Charitable Services Business Worksheet (DLCP)

## What counsel must do before any filing

1. **Re-verify every legal citation** against current authority (the Internal Revenue Code, Treasury Regulations, Rev. Rul. 85-1, and the Form 1023 Instructions revision in effect on the filing date).
2. **Confirm form names and fees**:
   - Form 1023 user fee per the then-current Rev. Proc. (annual; currently $600)
   - Delaware Certificate of Incorporation fee per the then-current DE Division of Corporations fee schedule (currently $109 base for exempt corporations)
   - DC charitable-services BBL fee per the then-current DLCP schedule (verify; founder's prompt budgeted $80 — current schedule likely higher)
   - Whether DC has reintroduced or renamed a "Form CRA-1" since drafting
3. **Confirm factual placeholders** marked `[TBD]`: EIN, formation date, board composition, founder home address, registered agent contract, banking, etc.
4. **Tailor Part IV of Form 1023** (Activities) to the Foundation's actual operations as of the filing date.
5. **Run the public-support-test projection** through a CPA before filing Form 1023 Part VII.
6. **Execute the rebuttable-presumption procedure** for the founder's compensation **before** filing Form 1023 (the procedure must be in place; the founder's actual salary should be set by independent directors prior to filing). See [`06_REASONABLE_COMPENSATION_MEMO.md`](./06_REASONABLE_COMPENSATION_MEMO.md).
