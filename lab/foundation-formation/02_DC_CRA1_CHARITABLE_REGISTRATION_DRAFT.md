## 2. Sequence of DC filings (recommended order)

Per DC's standard new-business steps (per DLCP "Steps to Obtain a Basic Business License"):

1. **Corporate registration & EIN.** Register the Delaware-formed AAO Foundation as a foreign nonstock corporation with the DLCP Corporations Division (CorpOnline) and obtain a Federal EIN from the IRS. *Cost: DC foreign-entity registration fee ≈ $80 (verify).*
2. **Certificate of Occupancy / Home Occupancy Permit.** Confirm zoning compliance for 1480 Chapin St NW. If the Foundation will use the address only as a registered office and conducts no public activity there, a Home Occupancy Permit or a simple business address use may suffice. Counsel to confirm. *Cost: minimal.*
3. **New Business Tax Registration (FR-500 via MyTax.dc.gov).** Even tax-exempt entities must register with the DC Office of Tax and Revenue. The Foundation will also file **Form FR-164** to request exemption from DC franchise tax (after federal determination).
4. **Basic Business License — Charitable Services category.** Submit the BBL Application (Steps 1–5, Sections A–D) with the **Charitable Services Business Worksheet** completed and attached, plus supporting documents (Articles, Bylaws, IRS determination or pending status, financials, IRS Form 990 if available). *Cost: see § 8.*
5. **Charitable Trust Registration with DC OAG (if applicable).** Confirm whether the DC OAG Charitable Trust Section maintains a separate registration database for solicitations; if so, register there as well. Counsel to confirm.
