## Section 4 — Statement of bona fide intent to use

Trademark Center auto-populates the required §1(b) verified statement. The substance of what you are swearing to:

> The applicant has a bona fide intention, and is entitled, to use the mark in commerce on or in connection with the identified goods/services. To the best of the signatory's knowledge and belief, no other persons, except, if applicable, concurrent users, have the right to use the mark in commerce, either in the identical form or in such near resemblance as to be likely, when used on or in connection with the goods/services of such other persons, to cause confusion or mistake, or to deceive. The signatory believes the applicant to be the owner of the mark sought to be registered. The facts set forth in the application are true. All statements made of the signatory's own knowledge are true and all statements made on information and belief are believed to be true.

## Section 10 — Submission flow in Trademark Center

1. Log in at https://trademarkcenter.uspto.gov/
2. Click **"File a new application"**
3. Select **"Trademark/Service Mark"** (not Certification Mark — see RISK_NOTES.md §1)
4. Mark type: **Standard character mark**
5. Enter literal element: **AAO-GOVERNED**
6. Add Class 042 from the ID Manual — search for "design and development of computer hardware and software"
7. Filing basis: **Section 1(b) — Intent to Use**
8. Owner info, signatory info, correspondence info (sections above)
9. Review and sign with `/John Bradley/`
10. Pay $350 by credit card or USPTO Financial Manager account
11. **Save the Serial Number** (assigned immediately upon successful submission) — you will need it for all future correspondence

Expected examination timeline: **8-12 months** to first Office Action under current USPTO backlog.
