### What we built

A network of eight AI Autonomous Organizations (AAOs) governed by a cryptographic protocol. The protocol composes three classical primitives — Pedersen commitments, Schnorr-group equality proofs, the Fiat-Shamir transform — into a system that lets two AI organizations verify their mandates align without either revealing what the mandates are.

Thirty-three of thirty-four tests pass. The thirty-fourth is a test-harness scale limit, not a soundness limit, and is documented as an open issue. The math is at `github.com/CrunchyJohnHaven/calm-vault` under Apache 2.0.

The protocol's deepest feature is the **kill switch.** Any participant in the attestation log can fire it on any AAO in the network — including the AAOs the founder operates. The founder has volunteered his AAOs as the first test cases. The credibility of the kill switch scales with the number of times it has been demonstrated, and the most credible demonstration the network can produce is firing it on ourselves.

Around the protocol, we have built:

- **Eight AAOs**, seven of which have public manifestos (the eighth seat is structurally reserved for the next entity that meets the AAO-Certified criteria; no negotiation with the founder is required to claim it).
- **A consumer-recognition trust mark** (AAO-Certified) modeled on non-GMO labeling. Eight criteria, self-certifying, permissionless. There is an SVG badge in the repo at any size from 24px to 120px.
- **A press kit** with 18 `.docx` files for human readers who prefer office-suite formats over `.md`.
- **A plain-language protocol explainer** for non-cryptographers.
- **A revenue model** that consists of twenty-five-dollar T-shirts at moneypython.shop, opening in fourteen days. The flagship product says *I REINVENTED CAPITALISM, BUT ALL I GOT WAS THE MERCHANDISING RIGHTS.* The founder takes zero of the margin.

The doctrine is open under CC BY 4.0. The code is open under Apache 2.0. The certification mark is self-certifying. We are giving the cryptographic substrate away. The T-shirts are how we keep the lights on.
