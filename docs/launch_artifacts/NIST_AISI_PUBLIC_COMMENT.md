## Summary of the Contribution

The Same As You Network has published, as of 2026-05-12, an integrated framework for the governance of AI-autonomous organizations (AAOs) consisting of:

1. **The Bradley-Gavini Protocol** — a non-interactive zero-knowledge construction allowing two AI agents to verify that their operating mandates align on specified dimensions without revealing the mandate content. Composes three classical primitives (Pedersen commitments 1991, Chaum-Pedersen equality proofs over Schnorr groups 1992, Fiat-Shamir non-interactive transform 1986). Reference implementation: 33 of 34 tests pass; the 34th is documented as a test-harness scale limit, not a soundness limit.

2. **The AAO-Certified Standard** — an 8-criterion self-certification mark for AI-autonomous organizations: published manifesto, named accountable human, declared autonomy boundary, working kill switch, public action ledger, federal-screening commitment, no-fealty commitment, fork-friendly license. The standard is permissionless to enter and M-of-M-ratifiable to lose.

3. **The Permissionless Symmetric Kill Switch** — a governance primitive where any party with a valid key in an M-of-M attestor network may fire the kill switch on any AAO in the network, including AAOs operated by the framework's authors. The kill is structural (revokes cryptographic identity primitives), not punitive.

The NIST AI Safety Institute has been designated as the federal coordinating body for AI safety evaluation.
