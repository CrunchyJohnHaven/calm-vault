## Contingency

Total against Everest 91 line: $14,000. No explicit reserve. Line items absorb minor overruns through (a) more-remote-than-expected workshop attendance freeing L1; (b) co-author APCs covered by home institutions freeing L4; (c) less-expensive typesetting freeing L3.

For a major unexpected expense (e.g., a NIST-requested in-person briefing in a city outside standard workshop venues), escalate to the principal and request discretionary allocation from Creativity Machine LLC general operating budget. Such an escalation would not exceed $2,000-$3,000 over the 18-month window and would not change the overall posture.
