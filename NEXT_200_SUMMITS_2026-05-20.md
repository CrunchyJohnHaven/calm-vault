## §6. What this list does NOT include

- **Standards-track adoption** is enumerated but not driven (CS-09 to CS-14 specify the submission; ratification is outside our timeline control).
- **Adoption case studies** (CS-20) are exit criteria, not entry criteria.
- **Future predicate vocabularies** beyond the v0 Compass set are intentionally absent — adding them is a CC-04 review, not a roadmap item.
- **Hardware co-design** (custom silicon for Bulletproofs) is out-of-scope; software path only.
- **AGI-readiness** (what happens when one of the agents becomes meaningfully smarter than its operator policy assumed) is acknowledged but unscheduled; CS-14 academic paper is the venue.
