# Research Participants Wanted

## Behavioral-Biometric Identity Study

We are looking for **15 adults** to take part in a 13-week research study on how well a new identity-verification system can recognize a person from their **handwriting** and **voice**. The study is run by [Partner Institution] in collaboration with Calm Research, under [Institution] IRB approval.

---

### What you'll do

- **One enrollment session (about 60 minutes)** at our research office or by video call. You'll write a few short prompts on an iPad with a stylus, record a few short voice samples, and answer some basic questions.
- **Twelve weekly sessions (15-20 minutes each)** that you complete at home, on your own schedule (with a 2-day flexibility window each week). You'll write one short prompt and record one short voice sample each week, using an app we provide.
- **One brief end-of-study session (30 minutes)** to wrap up.

**Total time over 13 weeks: about 4-5 hours.**

---

### What gets collected

This study has been designed so your raw handwriting and voice recordings **never leave your device**. The app computes a single similarity score on your iPad and sends only the score to us — not the actual writing or audio. You can verify this yourself in the open-source app code.

---

### Compensation

- **$50** when you finish the enrollment session
- **$30** per weekly session (up to $360)
- **$60** completion bonus for finishing at least 10 of the 12 weekly sessions
- **Up to $470 total**

Paid by gift card or direct deposit, your choice. Paid promptly after each session.

---

### Who can participate

- Adults **18 or older**
- Comfortable writing a short paragraph by hand and speaking for about a minute
- Able to commit to 12 weekly check-ins over 13 weeks
- Speak [English / French / German depending on site]

We particularly welcome participants who:
- Are over 50 or under 25 (we need a balanced age range)
- Are left-handed writers (under-represented in similar studies)
- Have non-English/French/German as a first language (we want to study generalization)

We **cannot enroll** anyone who is currently a Calm employee or contractor, or whose primary writing hand is injured at the time of enrollment.

---

### Privacy and your rights

- You can withdraw at any time without giving a reason. We pay you for every session you completed.
- Within 30 days of withdrawal, we erase your records from our database.
- Aggregate, anonymized results will be published in a scientific journal. Your individual results will not be published.
- The study is reviewed by [Local IRB / Research Ethics Board], reference [REB-XXXX].
