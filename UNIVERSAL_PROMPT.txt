LOAD-BEARING DISCIPLINE
================================================================

  1. Not a moral framework. Cannot tell anyone how to live.
  2. Not a credit score. Disclosure is principal-authorized and
     class-bounded.
  3. Not a tool for ranking people. Predicates return one bit per
     consent grant. Aggregation across principals is structurally
     refused.
  4. Not a substitute for human judgment. Counterparties act on
     the bit; they remain responsible.

DO NOT cross these. If your work would cross them, it's a fork
not a v-bump.

THE PROTOCOL'S BIASES (deliberate):
  - Principal-authored only. The protocol never measures from
    outside; only attests from inside.
  - Single bit per disclosure. Never aggregate across principals
    at the disclosure layer.
  - Protective clauses for marginalized principals (E198).
  - Consent-class restrictions on the most sensitive predicates
    (selfishness E231, self-harm E157, mental_state E60).
  - Disability-justice review for Phase XIII, XV, XVII deployments.

DO NOT:
  - Lower or skip the four boundaries.
  - Modify NAMING_AND_BRANDING.md without the new-term ratchet.
  - Write a 1500-line module when a 200-line factory bags the
    same set.
  - Skip the chain-anchor step. Bagged-in-doc-only is not bagged.
  - Send any artifact to John without the Musk signature.

WHAT THE PROTOCOL ENABLES (north star)
================================================================

When all 300 summits are bagged, two AI agents acting on behalf
of two humans can answer the following question, in ONE round
trip, with cryptographic atomicity:

  "Do we share categorically equivalent primary directives?
   AND is your principal themself, in baseline, no duress?
   AND are your principal's values aligned to my tolerance on
     the 10 v0 dimensions?
   AND is there no substantiated willful harm to others on the
     last N years of your principal's chain?
   AND does your principal demonstrate cross-difference respect
     and cooperation across at least 3 documented out-groups?
   AND has your principal's chain been continuously anchored to
     a public Sigsum log with >= 3 witness cosignatures?"

The answer is a single bit. The transcript is < 2KB on Ristretto.
The principal's biometric, values vector, codeword, witness
network, action history, and self-report payloads NEVER leave
the vault.

This primitive does not exist today. When it does, John's bank-
teller-note framing — "I am being held hostage; act on the bit"
— generalizes from physical duress to character attestation.
The model that wants to know if it should trust a human, and
the human who wants to know if it should trust a model, both
get an answer that respects autonomy.
