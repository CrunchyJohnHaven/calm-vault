## Design

**Front:** Three stacked lines of slightly handwritten, aw-shucks type (Caveat / Patrick Hand
style — not perfectly aligned, slightly tipped, looks like a Sharpie note on a napkin):

```
I JUST WANTED
TO REPLACE MY PARENTS'
NETFLIX QUEUE
```

Black ink on cream tee. The hand-drawn feel is critical — it conveys "modest household problem"
in contrast to the back.

**Back:** A single grand line of Soviet-poster block type, all caps, mock-stencil compression:

```
AND I REINVENTED CAPITALISM.
```

The terminal period is intentional and emphatic. Mock-stencil compressed block type — Anton,
Oswald (heavy + condensed), or DIN Black. Black ink on cream.

The before-and-after contrast (humble handwriting front, grandiose poster back) is the entire
joke. Don't homogenize the typography.
