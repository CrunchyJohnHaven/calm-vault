Pre-upload checklist (per design)

  • Convert text to paths before uploading to Printful — eliminates font fallback risk. From the repo root:
    inkscape designs/13-front.svg --export-text-to-path --export-filename=designs/13-front-paths.svg
    ...repeat for each SVG. Or do it in Affinity Designer: File → Convert to Curves.
  • Verify print size — Printful expects the artwork to fill the print area you select. The 4500×5400 viewBox already matches Printful's max DTG area (15"×18").
  • Confirm print color: white-on-black for Products 13 + 16, black-on-cream for Product 18. The SVGs encode this correctly.
  • For Product 16 back: ship the -stub.svg at launch. Replace with Flux output when quota resets. See designs/16-back-flux-prompt.md.
